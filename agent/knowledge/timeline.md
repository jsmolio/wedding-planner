# Wedding Planning Timeline

## 12+ Months Before
- Set your overall budget and decide who is contributing financially.
- Create your guest list draft — this drives almost every other decision.
- Research and book your ceremony and reception venue.
- Start researching photographers, videographers, and caterers.

## 10-12 Months Before
- Book your photographer, videographer, and caterer.
- Choose your wedding party (bridesmaids, groomsmen, etc.).
- Start shopping for wedding attire.
- Research officiants and book one.

## 8-10 Months Before
- Send save-the-date cards.
- Book a florist and discuss seasonal flower options.
- Book music — DJ or live band.
- Plan your honeymoon and book travel.

## 6-8 Months Before
- Order wedding invitations and stationery.
- Book hair and make-up artists for a trial session.
- Register for gifts.
- Start planning your ceremony readings and vows.

## 3-4 Months Before
- Send formal invitations (allow 6-8 weeks for RSVPs).
- Order your wedding cake.
- Arrange transportation for the wedding day.
- Purchase wedding favours and accessories.
- Schedule your final dress fitting.

## 1-2 Months Before
- Confirm all vendor details and delivery times.
- Create a detailed day-of timeline for the wedding party and vendors.
- Apply for your marriage license.
- Write your vows if you are writing them yourself.

## 2-3 Weeks Before
- Finalise the seating chart and place cards.
- Confirm final guest headcount with the caterer.
- Break in your wedding shoes.
- Prepare tips and thank-you gifts for vendors and the wedding party.

## Day Before
- Attend the rehearsal and rehearsal dinner.
- Lay out everything you need for the next day.
- Get a good night's sleep!
